why Thaagam ?

Thaagam Foundation operates with 100% transparency, ensuring that every donation directly supports those in need. Our mission is to alleviate hunger and uplift communities through food, education, and healthcare, focusing on long-term solutions that create lasting change. We provide regular updates, allowing donors to see the tangible impact of their contributions. Together, we can transform lives and build a brighter future.

Educate a Child

Child Education Since December 2021, Thaagam Foundation’s Child Education program has been a vital support system for economically disadvantaged children in Delhi and Noida. With the help of generous donors, the program has raised ₹49,45,572 from 3,511 donors through 6,816 donations. This initiative focuses on providing essential resources such as tuition fees, books, and other educational materials, enabling children to continue their education without financial burdens. By addressing these challenges, the program has significantly contributed to improving access to quality education for thousands of students. School Bag Donation Launched in May 2024, the School Bag Donation program aims to encourage school attendance by easing financial constraints on families. Focused primarily in Delhi and Noida, this initiative has raised ₹1,30,025 from 86 donors, enabling the distribution of 260 school bags filled with essential supplies, including stationery and uniforms. These donations have not only motivated students to attend school regularly but have also inspired confidence and excitement for learning among young children. Crowdfund Education The Crowdfund Education program, launched in April 2024, connects donors directly with underprivileged students to ensure targeted and impactful support. Operating mainly in Chennai, the program has already raised ₹10,18,237 from 698 donors, benefiting 109 students. By focusing on personalized aid, such as covering tuition fees and other educational needs, Crowdfund Education empowers students to pursue their academic goals without interruption. This initiative highlights Thaagam Foundation’s commitment to fostering a brighter future through education.
